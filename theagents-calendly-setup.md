# The Agents - Calendly Configuration

## Required Calendly Event Types

### 1. Demo 15 min
**URL:** https://calendly.com/theagents/demo-15min
**Purpose:** Quick product demonstration
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Description:** "Descubrí cómo nuestros agentes IA pueden transformar tu negocio en solo 15 minutos. Te mostramos casos reales y calculamos tu ROI específico."

**Questions to ask:**
- Company name
- Industry  
- Main challenge you want to solve
- Team size
- Best number to call

**Meeting details:**
- Platform: Google Meet (auto-generated)
- Buffer time: 5 min before, 5 min after
- Available: Mon-Fri 9am-6pm Argentina time
- Max meetings per day: 8

### 2. Strategy Session 30 min  
**URL:** https://calendly.com/theagents/strategy-30min
**Purpose:** Deep dive strategy consultation
**Duration:** 30 minutes
**Description:** "Sesión estratégica para diseñar tu roadmap completo de transformación con IA. Incluye audit de procesos actuales y plan de implementación personalizado."

**Questions to ask:**
- Company name and website
- Your role in the company
- Current team size
- Biggest business challenge
- Monthly revenue range
- What specific processes want to automate
- Decision timeline
- Budget range

**Meeting details:**
- Platform: Google Meet
- Buffer time: 10 min before, 5 min after  
- Available: Tue-Thu 10am-5pm Argentina time
- Max meetings per day: 4 (more focused sessions)

### 3. Urgent Consultation
**URL:** https://calendly.com/theagents/urgent-consultation
**Purpose:** Same-day problem solving
**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Description:** "¿Necesitás resolver algo urgente con IA? Hablemos hoy mismo. Sesión express para pain points críticos."

**Questions to ask:**
- Company name
- Urgent issue description
- When do you need this solved
- Contact number

**Meeting details:**
- Available: Mon-Fri 9am-7pm (same day booking)
- Buffer time: 5 min before
- Max meetings per day: 3

## Integration with Instagram
- All Calendly links should be tracked with UTM parameters:
  - Source: instagram
  - Medium: social
  - Campaign: theagents-automation
  - Content: [post-id]

Example: https://calendly.com/theagents/demo-15min?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=theagents-automation&utm_content=post1-5tareas
