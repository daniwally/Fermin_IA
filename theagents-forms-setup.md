# The Agents - Lead Capture Forms Setup

## Form 1: ROI Calculator
**URL target:** https://forms.gle/theagents-roi-calculator
**Purpose:** Calculate potential ROI from IA implementation
**Trigger:** Post #2 - "¿Cuánto cuesta NO tener IA?"

### Questions:
1. **Company name** (text)
2. **Industry** (dropdown: SaaS, E-commerce, Real Estate, Marketing Agency, Consulting, Other)
3. **Company size** (dropdown: 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 200+)
4. **Monthly leads received** (number)
5. **Current lead response time** (dropdown: <1hr, 1-24hrs, 1-3 days, 3+ days)
6. **Average lead value** (number)
7. **Main pain points** (checkboxes: slow follow-up, manual processes, lead scoring, reporting, competitor tracking)
8. **Contact info** (email, phone)
9. **Best time to discuss results** (text)

## Form 2: Team Assessment  
**URL target:** https://forms.gle/theagents-team-assessment
**Purpose:** Assess team readiness for IA transformation
**Trigger:** Post #3 - "Agente IA vs Empleado"

### Questions:
1. **Company name** (text)
2. **Your role** (dropdown: CEO/Founder, Sales Manager, Operations, Marketing, Other)
3. **Team size** (number)
4. **Biggest productivity challenge** (text)
5. **Hours/week spent on repetitive tasks** (dropdown: <5, 5-15, 15-30, 30+)
6. **Current automation level** (scale 1-5: manual → fully automated)
7. **Team tech-savviness** (scale 1-5: low → high)
8. **Priority areas for automation** (checkboxes: sales, marketing, customer service, operations, reporting)
9. **Budget range for transformation** (dropdown: <$5K, $5K-15K, $15K-50K, $50K+)
10. **Contact info** (email, phone)

## Form 3: IA Implementation Audit
**URL target:** https://forms.gle/theagents-ia-audit  
**Purpose:** Audit current IA implementation issues
**Trigger:** Post #4 - "3 errores con IA"

### Questions:
1. **Company name** (text)
2. **Current IA tools used** (checkboxes: ChatGPT, Zapier, HubSpot, Salesforce AI, Custom solution, None)
3. **Implementation timeframe** (dropdown: No IA yet, <6 months, 6-12 months, 1+ years)
4. **Main implementation challenges** (checkboxes: No strategy, Poor adoption, No training, No ROI, Technical issues)
5. **Team satisfaction with current IA** (scale 1-5: very unsatisfied → very satisfied)
6. **Biggest missed opportunity** (text)
7. **Priority for fixing** (dropdown: Strategy, Training, Technology, Process, ROI measurement)
8. **Decision timeline** (dropdown: Immediate, 1 month, 3 months, 6+ months)
9. **Contact info** (email, phone)
10. **Preferred communication** (dropdown: email, WhatsApp, phone call, video call)
