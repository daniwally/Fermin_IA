- **Sales Cycle:** 45-60 días

- **Profile:** Established SMEs seeking digital transformation
- **Pain Points:**

- **Impact:** Need broader stakeholder buy-in

#### **Innovation Appetite:**
- **Chile:** Government-supported innovation culture
- **Argentina:** Entrepreneurial risk-taking spirit
- **Impact:** Different value propositions needed

#### **Chile Advantages:**
- Government support (InvestChile, StartUp Chile)
- Strong fintech/agri-tech foundation
- International mindset
- Better infrastructure

#### **Market Sophistication:**

- More sophisticated procurement processes
- Established vendor relationships
- Premium pricing acceptance
